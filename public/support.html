Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new diary entry?

Tap the pencil icon in the top right corner of the main screen. Fill in the title, date, and your entry content, then tap "Save".

How do I edit an existing entry?

Tap on any diary entry to open it, then tap the pencil icon to edit. Make your changes and tap "Update" to save.

How do I delete an entry?

Open the diary entry you want to delete, then tap the trash icon. Confirm the deletion when prompted. Note: This action cannot be undone.

Is my diary private?

Yes! Your diary entries are only accessible by you when logged into your account. We do not read, share, or sell your personal content.

How do I change the app theme?

On the login screen, use the colored circles at the bottom to switch themes. On the main screen, tap your profile icon and select "Theme" to change it.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Currently, password reset is not available. Please contact us for assistance with account recovery.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, please contact us at the email below. We will process your request and delete all associated data.
