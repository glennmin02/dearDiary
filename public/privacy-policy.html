Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Dear Diary ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username: Used to identify your account
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely, never in plain text

Diary Content

  • Diary entries: The titles, content, and dates you provide
  • This content is stored securely and is only accessible by you

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Dear Diary service
  • Authenticate your account
  • Store and display your diary entries

Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Vercel and Neon (PostgreSQL)
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your diary content with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track your activity for analytics

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You can delete your diary entries at any time through the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your diary entries
  • Request account deletion by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Dear Diary is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
